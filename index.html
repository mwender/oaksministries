Live ABOVE the Chaos

Are you finding it increasingly difficult to navigate within this changing world?

You are not alone.

When life around us keeps changing direction, it can become difficult to strengthen a relationship with God and live a life of stability and purpose. We can veer away from who we imagined ourselves becoming and instead become controlled by circumstances that come our way without rhyme or reason. When this happens, we can confuse the people around us as much as we confuse ourselves!

Oaks Ministries is dedicated to helping people grow in their ability to display God’s nature to a watching world no matter what.

Start from wherever you are.

Spiritual journeys often meander like a winding road through the countryside — up and down hills and around sharp bends without guardrails or lighting. It can take years to sort through our life experiences, what we have been taught, and what the bible says. This website provides space for wherever you are on your spiritual journey. Spend some time examining the resources available and consider seizing opportunities to connect with others so you are not trying to grow in your faith alone.

You are not alone.
